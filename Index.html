Introduction

Purpose

This implementation guide describes the the lesion management information model and API interaction that are used by the AIR board application.

Scope

The information contained in this document consists of FHIR profiles with example resources instances, a capability statement resource, example value sets.

Intended audience

This documentation is directly useable by the PD-EDI and IntelliSpace and Vue PACS product team to take as input.

The AI Results Board

AIR is the environment used by the radiologist to gather (AI) information in a structured way and prepare the report. Both the output of AI models, and the user input are captured in FHIR resources and stored in the CDR. The lesion and its features are modeled as independent entities, thus allowing tracking lesions over multiple studies and their evolution over time. The gathered structured data acts as a source for reporting, avoiding double input by radiologists, and also serves as feedback to retrain AI models and monitor their performance in the clinical setting.
